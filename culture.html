Religion

Distribution of faith in Bangladesh

  • Muslim: 90%
  • Hindu: 9%
  • Others: 1%

The majority of people in Bangladesh are Muslim. About 10 percent of Bangladeshis are Hindus and the remaining 1% are mainly Christians and Buddhists.4

Food

Bangladeshi Food

historyplex.com

Bangladeshi food is very regional, in the areas of Rajshahi and Rangpur people eat a lot of freshwater fish, whereas in Barisal and Khulna saltwater fish is more common. Much of the bangladeshi diet consists of the following:5

  • Rice
  • Vegetables
  • Meats such as goat, beef and fish
