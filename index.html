The War

Bangladesh gained their independence after the Bangladesh Liberation War of 1971. The war was fought between the 26th of March1 and the 16th of December.2

26th of March

The Proclamation of Bangladeshi Independence was created on the 26th of March 19711 and marks the official Independence Day of Bangladesh.

16th of December

The 16th of December is recogniserd as Victory Day in Bangladesh.3 This day marks the day Bangladesh won the war for independence and is celebrated as a national holiday.
